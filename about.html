About The Insitute

Elite Computer Academy has a dedicated team of skilled teachers and counselors to make sure the regular up gradation of curriculum and quality delivery along with career guidance. Our professional team works harmoniously in unison, happily pushing the frontiers growth and quality in imparting education.Elite Computer Academy is steadily surging ahead as an eminent centre of excellence for computer professionals and job oriented education. This seat of higher learning is not just devoted to impart and spread quality education but also strives to ensure quality life to one and all.

Elite Computer Academy is one of the largest IT & Electronics as well as General Competition training institutes in India that trains youth and students in multiple segments of related education. The wide Spread network of our centers is the mark of quality education, training and professional certifications that serve the Information Technology needs of the students and the industry.

Satya ITI Muzaffarpur is an Industrial Training Institute affiliated to Skill Development and Industrial Training Department Bihar & DGT Govt. of India, Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (NCVET) . There are three most valuable technical courses in our ITI like Electrician, Wireman & Fitter .
